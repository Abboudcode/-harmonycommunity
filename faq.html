Questions people usually ask

Here are quick answers so you know exactly what Harmony is – and what it isn’t.

Is Harmony completely anonymous?

In the full app you’ll be able to choose: post with your real name, a nickname, or as “Anonymous”. Your feelings stay real either way.

Is Harmony a replacement for therapy?

No. Harmony is a community space for support and understanding, not medical or professional advice. It can walk with you, not replace a doctor or therapist.

Will people judge me if my story sounds small?

Here, we believe if it keeps you awake at night, it is big enough. Harmony is designed to reduce shame, not increase it.

When will the real app be ready?

This site is an early preview. I’m building Harmony step by step. If you like the idea, your feedback and email will help decide the next features.
